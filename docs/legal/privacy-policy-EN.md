## 1. Who this policy covers

Cram Journey is primarily used by children; accounts are created and managed by a parent or legal guardian ("Parent"). Any data relating to a child is collected only after the Parent completes the verification procedure in Section 4.

If you are in Hong Kong, this policy is interpreted under the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance (Cap. 486). For users in the US, EU, UK, or elsewhere, we aim to follow applicable children's privacy principles (e.g. COPPA, UK Age Appropriate Design Code). *[This sentence requires qualified legal review before international launch — it is not a guarantee of compliance in any jurisdiction.]*

## 2. What we collect

| Category | Examples | How collected |
|---|---|---|
| Parent account | Name, email, optional phone | Registration |
| Child profile | Nickname, grade/age band | Parent creates profile |
| Learning behaviour | Response times, accuracy, replays, Memory Strength scores | Automatic during use |
| Uploaded materials | Photos or text of study content | User upload |
| Device / technical | Device model, app version, error logs | Automatic |

We do **not** intentionally collect a child's full legal name (unless the Parent voluntarily provides it), home address, school name, or identity documents.

---

## 3. How we use data

- Core App features: Memory Strength, daily review scheduling, game content
- Uploaded materials may be sent to **Google Gemini API** for content analysis to generate questions. This transfer is necessary for the App to function and is **not** used for advertising. *(We will review third-party API terms periodically and update this section if they change.)*
- Maintenance, debugging, and product improvement
- We do **not** sell personal data or use children's learning data for advertising

---

## 4. Parental consent (verifiable)

Because children use the App, we use a two-step consent process:

**Step 1 — Account creation:** When a Parent registers, they must confirm they have read and agree to this Privacy Policy and Terms. This alone does **not** authorize collection of a child's learning data.

**Step 2 — Before a child profile is active:** Before any learning data is saved, we email the Parent's registered address a one-time confirmation link. The child's profile remains **pending** — no learning data is persisted — until the Parent clicks that link.

Parents may withdraw consent at any time via in-app settings or by emailing Section 11. Withdrawal triggers deletion as described in Section 6.
